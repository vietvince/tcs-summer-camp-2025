## Getting Started with Scratch üö©

### Step 1. Remove the Default Sprite

1. In the Sprites pane, right-click the default **Cat** sprite.  
2. Select **delete** to clear the stage for your game.

### Step 2. Upload the Space Backdrop

:::info
<details style={{border:'none'}}>
    <summary>**Coach Note!**</summary>

Backdrops set the scene for your game. Choose a high-contrast space image so your sprites stand out!
</details>
:::

1. Click the **Stage** thumbnail, then the **Backdrops** tab.  
2. Click **Upload Backdrop**, select your space backdrop image.  
3. Rename the backdrop to **Space Scene** by clicking its name in the Backdrops list.

### Step 3. Import the Dot Sprite

1. Click **Choose a Sprite** ‚Üí **Upload Sprite**.  
2. Select a player sprite of your choice.  
3. Rename this sprite to **Dot**.  
4. In the **Costumes** tab, center and scale it so it‚Äôs about 15‚Äì20% of the stage height.

### Step 4. Import the Star Sprite

1. Click **Choose a Sprite** ‚Üí **Upload Sprite**.  
2. Select a star of your choice.
3. Rename this sprite to **Star**.  
4. Center and scale the costume so it‚Äôs about 10‚Äì15% of the stage height.

### Step 5. Import the Crystal Sprite

1. Click **Choose a Sprite** ‚Üí **Upload Sprite**.  
2. Select a crystal of your choice.
3. Rename this sprite to **Crystal**.  
4. Center and scale the costume so it‚Äôs about 10‚Äì15% of the stage height.

### Step 6. Arrange and Hide

1. In the Sprites pane, verify the order (top to bottom):  
   - Dot  
   - Star  
   - Crystal  
2. Click the **eye** icon next to **Star** and **Crystal** to hide them for now.

### Step 7. Save Your Progress

- Click **File ‚Üí Save now** to lock in your work.
